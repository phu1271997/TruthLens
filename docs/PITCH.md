# TruthLens: 60-Second Pitch Script

**Slide 1: The Hook (15s)**
Misinformation isn't just annoying; it costs the world 78 billion dollars every year. Existing solutions like X Community Notes take days to verify claims, by which time the damage is already done. And traditional smart contracts can't solve this, because they can't handle subjective truth. 

**Slide 2: The Solution (15s)**
Enter TruthLens: Free speech, without free lies. It's a decentralized social network where every viral post is fact-checked by AI consensus *before* it reaches a wide audience. Users stake TRUTH tokens to post. If the AI Jury verifies their claim, they double their money. If they lie, their stake is burned, and the post is permanently flagged.

**Slide 3: Why GenLayer? (15s)**
This is only possible on GenLayer. We use GenLayer Intelligent Contracts to actively crawl the web—Reuters, AP, Wikipedia—without centralized oracles. Then, we use the consensus layer LLMs to process that evidence and reach a subjective verdict. It takes seconds, not days.

**Slide 4: The Ask (15s)**
We've built a fully functional MVP and deployed our Intelligent Contracts to the GenLayer testnet. We are TruthLens, and we're bringing economic consequences to online truth. Try the demo today.
